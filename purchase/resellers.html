Reseller Information


If you are a reseller, please contact us beforehand via email. Our license normally prohibits transferring licenses - we will need to know the end client beforehand to make an exception here.

Payment Terms
We accept all major credit cards, PayPal an wire transfer. Wire usually takes a few days and we have to wait for proof of payment to process the purchase. The simplest way is using our purchase website with CC or PayPal. We can create an offer for a write transfer, but that usually takes a bit.

Discounts
We offer discounts if you require multiple licenses. For a single license, our website prices apply.

Quotes
Our license is structured in a way that most businesses probably need or want a custom contract depending on the complexity of their app. If you're unsure about your requirements, make your client contact us so we figure out the specific needs before continuing on the purchase.

Delivery
After the purchase went through, or license server sends an automated email to the email address you used for purchasing. Thus, it is important that you already use the client email address here. If you have to use yours, forward ALL of the fulfilment emails to your client, so they can register on our server. Also send us an email so we can update the record to the client's email address.
