Reseller Information

If you are a reseller, please continue us beforehand. Our license normally prohibits transferring licenses - we will need to know the end client beforehand to make an exception here.

Payment Terms/b>

We accept all major credit cards, PayPal an wire transfer. Wire usually takes a few days and we have to wait for proof of payment to process the purchase.

Discounts

We offer discounts if you require multiple licenses. For a single license, our website prices apply.

Delivery

After the purchase went through, or license server sends an automated email to the email address you used for purchasing. Thus, it is important that you already use the client email address here. If you have to use yours, forward ALL of the fulfillment emails to your client, so they can register on our server. Also send us an email so we can update the record to the client's email address.
